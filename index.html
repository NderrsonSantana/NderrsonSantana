Who We Are

A New Era of Construction

Located in the bustling heart of São Paulo, Construcao e Reforma Novo Design Ltda is a construction firm dedicated to both new builds and the intelligent revitalization of existing structures.

Under the leadership of Anderson Oliveira Santana, we specialize in the nonresidential sector. We understand that modern businesses need spaces that are not only structurally sound but also adaptable and aesthetically current. Our "Novo Design" philosophy integrates contemporary construction techniques with efficient renovation strategies.

From industrial facility upgrades to complete commercial fit-outs, we bring a fresh perspective to every project site in São Paulo.
